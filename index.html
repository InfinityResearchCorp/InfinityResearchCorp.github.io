Project mission

Our mission is to explore space, study and implement new methods of observing space objects. The project will include open instructions, diagrams and drawings for self-assembly of budget radio telescopes, as well as software for configuring and controlling these devices. In addition, a global network will be created for sharing data from radio telescopes, creating a unified and high-performance system. We will also actively explore new technologies for more optimal and accurate observation of space and space objects.
